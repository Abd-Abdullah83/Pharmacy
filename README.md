## ✨ Overview

**MediPharm** is a professional, production-quality **online pharmacy website** built entirely with **vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — zero frameworks, zero dependencies. It simulates a complete Pakistani e-pharmacy experience with multi-page navigation, a prescription upload system, a shopping cart, patient dashboard, and much more.

Designed with a clean, trust-first aesthetic inspired by modern healthcare platforms, MediPharm is a showcase of what's possible with pure frontend technologies.

## ⚕️ Healthcare Compliance Features

MediPharm was designed with **real-world pharmacy regulations** in mind:

- 🏛️ **DRAP License** number displayed prominently
- 📋 **Prescription-only (Rx)** medicines blocked without Rx upload
- ⚠️ **Medical disclaimers** on every product page and blog
- 🚨 **Emergency notice** — clearly not a substitute for emergency services
- 🔒 **PDPA compliance** language in privacy policy and upload flow
- 👨‍⚕️ **PharmD verification** messaging for prescription review
- 📜 **PMDC registration** requirements in prescription upload instructions

## 🌍 Target Market Context

Built specifically for the **Pakistani healthcare market**:

- Prices displayed in **Pakistani Rupees (Rs.)**
- Emergency numbers: **1122 (Rescue)** and **115 (Edhi Foundation)**
- Regulatory body: **DRAP** (Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan)
- Doctor registration: **PMDC** (Pakistan Medical & Dental Council)
- Data protection: **PDPA** (Personal Data Protection Act)
- Delivery coverage: Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan
