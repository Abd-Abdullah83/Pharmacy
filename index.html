Panadol Extra Tablets
Generic Name: Paracetamol 500mg + Caffeine 65mg
OTC — No Prescription Required
Rs. 95 Rs. 120
Composition

Each tablet: Paracetamol 500mg, Caffeine 65mg. Excipients: Maize starch, Powdered cellulose, Pregelatinised starch, Stearic acid.

Uses / Indications

Relief of mild to moderate pain including headache, migraine, dental pain, period pain, backache, and rheumatic pain. Also reduces fever.

Manufacturer

Getz Pharma (Pvt.) Ltd., Karachi, Pakistan. Batch: GP-2024-X7 · Mfg: Jan 2024 · Exp: Jan 2026

⚠️ Important: Dosage information below is for reference only and must not replace your doctor's or pharmacist's specific instructions for your condition.
Adults & Children Over 12

1–2 tablets every 4–6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 8 tablets in 24 hours or take more frequently than every 4 hours.

Children Under 12

Not recommended. Use an age-appropriate formulation under medical supervision only.

Special Populations

Caution in liver or kidney impairment. Consult your pharmacist or physician before use.

Common Side Effects

Nausea, stomach upset, skin rash (rare). The caffeine component may cause mild insomnia or nervousness in caffeine-sensitive individuals.

Serious Warnings

Do not use if you have liver disease, kidney disease, or are taking other paracetamol-containing products. Overdose causes serious liver damage — seek emergency care immediately.

Storage Conditions

Store below 25°C in a dry place. Protect from direct sunlight and moisture. Keep out of reach of children. Do not use after the expiry date printed on the pack.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services, prescriptions, and deliveries

How do I upload my prescription? ▼
Go to the "Upload Prescription" page from the navigation menu or click the green "Upload Rx" button in the header. We accept JPG, PNG, PDF, and HEIC formats. Take a clear photo of your doctor's prescription and upload it. Our pharmacist will review it within 30 minutes during business hours (9 AM–10 PM) and instantly during emergencies.
Can I order medicines without a prescription? ▼
Yes! Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines — marked with a green "OTC" badge — can be ordered freely without any prescription. Products marked with a red "Rx Required" badge are Schedule-H / prescription-only medicines under DRAP regulations and require a valid prescription from a PMDC-registered doctor before dispensing.
How long does delivery take? ▼
We offer same-day delivery within Lahore for orders placed before 3:00 PM (add-on fee of Rs. 50). Next-day delivery is available across major cities including Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, and Multan. You'll receive a real-time tracking link via SMS once your order is dispatched.
Are your medicines 100% genuine? ▼
Absolutely. MediPharm is a DRAP-licensed pharmacy (License No: PB-PH-2024-001) and all medicines are sourced directly from DRAP-verified manufacturers or their authorized distributors. We maintain strict cold-chain management for temperature-sensitive medicines and conduct regular quality checks. No counterfeits or substitutions — ever.
What is your refund and return policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day return policy on sealed, unopened medicines. Once a medicine's tamper-evident seal is broken, it cannot be returned for safety and hygiene reasons. For wrong, damaged, or expired deliveries, we offer a 100% refund or same-day replacement — no questions asked. Contact us within 24 hours of delivery for fastest resolution.
How is my prescription and health data protected? ▼
Your prescription and all health information are protected by 256-bit SSL encryption and stored on secure, PDPA-compliant servers. Access is strictly limited to our licensed pharmacists on a need-to-know basis. We never sell, share, or disclose your health data to any third parties, advertisers, or government bodies except where required by law.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), Cash on Delivery (COD) across all serviceable cities, and Mobile Wallets (JazzCash, EasyPaisa). All online transactions are processed through a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway with 3D Secure authentication.

Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Eligibility for Returns

You may return sealed, unopened medicines within 7 days of delivery. Products must be in their original, tamper-evident sealed packaging. Medicines with broken seals, opened packaging, or requiring cold chain storage cannot be returned for safety and hygiene reasons.

2. Wrong or Damaged Deliveries

If you receive the wrong product, a damaged product, or a product past its expiry date, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange a same-day replacement or issue a 100% refund to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.

3. Refund Process

Refunds are processed to your original payment method. Card refunds typically take 3-7 business days. COD refunds are processed via bank transfer or mobile wallet within 2 business days of the return being received.

Prescription medicines dispensed against a valid Rx, temperature-sensitive products (vaccines, insulin), and opened personal care products cannot be refunded under any circumstances.
